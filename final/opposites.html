Opposites

This project called for a series of two posters: one image based and one typographic. Both of the posters display a pair of opposites. I chose the words focus and distraction to talk about how women are viewed as sexual objects in all aspects of life, even in the professional world. I also wanted to bring to light that women are often surpressed because of this and are viewed at as a distraction to men.
