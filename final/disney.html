100 Disney Characters Infographic

This project focuses on the collection and organization of data through seven different attributes. Once I chose 100 Disney characters it was clear I had a lot of information to work with due to Disney's intricate character development process. Each character has a colored bar underneith their picture that indicates their species, a number that relates to their location either on the map or in the fictional realms list to the right of the map, their role in Television of Films, and their destiny as a character as a hero, sidekick or villain.
