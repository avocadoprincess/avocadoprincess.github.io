Imagine Boston 2030 Campaign

Imagine Boston 2030 is a city planning campaign hoping to collab with the people of Boston and it's organizations to improve transportation, housing, environment, public spaces, prosperity, arts & culture, land use, higher education and safety of our city. By joining this campaign and sharing your vision of what you want Boston to be by 2030 we can assure ourselves and others of a bright future. This particular campaign approach targets the largest percentage of people in Boston, college students. Over 250,000 students walk the streets every day and what better way to make a difference than with the future leaders of our city. For more information about how to get involved go to imagine.boston.gov
